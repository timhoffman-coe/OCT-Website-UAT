## Growing the Team

March 2026 brought exciting changes to the Network Services and Connecting Technologies team, with two new additions and a well-deserved promotion that reflect OCT's investment in both new talent and internal career growth.

### Ali Younes — Network Services

Ali Younes joins the team bringing over a decade of IT experience and specialized certifications in Cisco and Fortinet technologies. His expertise in network infrastructure and security will strengthen the team's capacity as the City continues to modernize its connectivity infrastructure.

### Yuvraj Sandhu — Co-op Student from NAIT

Yuvraj Sandhu joins as a Co-op student from the Northern Alberta Institute of Technology (NAIT). Bringing in co-op students is part of OCT's ongoing commitment to fostering new talent within the municipal technology sector. These placements give students real-world experience in enterprise-scale networking while bringing fresh perspectives to the team.

### Cesar Uyenos — Promoted to Applications and Infrastructure Analyst 2

After **13 years of dedicated service** to the City of Edmonton, Cesar Uyenos has been promoted to Applications and Infrastructure Analyst 2. Cesar's deep expertise in managing complex infrastructure — including Cisco ACI fabrics — has made him an invaluable member of the team.

His promotion is a testament to the value of internal expertise and long-term institutional knowledge. Congratulations, Cesar.

### Building for the Future

These additions reflect a team that is growing strategically. Experienced professionals like Ali bring immediate capability, co-op students like Yuvraj ensure a pipeline of future talent, and promotions like Cesar's demonstrate that dedication and expertise are recognized and rewarded within OCT.
