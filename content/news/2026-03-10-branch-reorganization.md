## A Structural Evolution

The Open City & Technology branch has officially completed its structural reorganization, marking a significant milestone in how the City of Edmonton delivers digital services to its residents and internal partners.

### What Changed

The reorganization aligns our teams around three core pillars:

- **Service Delivery Excellence** — Ensuring reliable, responsive technology services across all municipal departments
- **Strategic Innovation** — Driving forward-looking investments in AI, cloud infrastructure, and data analytics
- **Partner Integration** — Deepening collaboration with city departments to understand and anticipate their technology needs

### Impact on Our Teams

Each section within OCT has been realigned to reduce handoff friction and improve accountability. The new structure empowers teams to own their outcomes end-to-end, from planning through delivery and ongoing support.

### Looking Ahead

With this reorganization in place, we're positioned to accelerate our digital transformation roadmap. Expect to see faster turnaround on partner requests, more proactive technology recommendations, and a stronger focus on measurable outcomes.
