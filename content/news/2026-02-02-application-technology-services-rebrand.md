## A New Identity for a New Era

Effective February 2026, the Business Solutions section within Open City & Technology has been officially renamed to **Application Technology Services (ATS)**. This change reflects the section's evolved mandate and its central role in delivering integrated, enterprise-level applications across the City of Edmonton.

The renaming was the result of collaborative input from teams across OCT, including Financial and Vendor Management and Business Intake. The decision was strategically timed to coincide with the start of the new budget year, giving the section a fresh organizational identity as we prepare for the next phase of service delivery.

### Why the Change?

The name "Business Solutions" served us well, but as our work has grown more technical and more deeply integrated with corporate systems, it no longer fully captured what we do. **Application Technology Services** better communicates:

- Our role as a provider of **enterprise application platforms**
- Our focus on **technical delivery and system integration**
- Our alignment with the branch's broader goal of achieving higher IT maturity by 2030

### What This Means for Our Partners

For departments and teams who work with us, the change is primarily in name. Your points of contact, service channels, and project workflows remain the same. What you can expect going forward is an even stronger emphasis on application quality, platform reliability, and proactive technology guidance.

### Looking Ahead

The ATS section enters this new chapter well-positioned. With the 2027-2030 budget cycle on the horizon and a renewed focus on IT governance and maturity, this rebranding is more than cosmetic — it's a signal that we are ready to meet the growing demands of municipal digital transformation.
