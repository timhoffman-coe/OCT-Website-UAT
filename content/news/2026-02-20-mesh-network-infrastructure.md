## Expanding Connectivity

The Technology Infrastructure Operations section has begun deploying next-generation mesh network nodes across civic facilities. This expansion supports both improved staff connectivity and the growing demand for IoT-enabled services.

### Why Mesh Networking

Traditional hub-and-spoke network topologies create single points of failure and bottlenecks. Mesh networking offers:

- **Redundancy** — If one node goes down, traffic automatically reroutes through neighboring nodes
- **Scalability** — New nodes can be added without reconfiguring the entire network
- **Lower Latency** — Data takes the shortest available path, improving response times for critical applications

### Deployment Timeline

The rollout is proceeding in three waves:

1. **Wave 1 (Complete)** — City Hall, Edmonton Tower, and Century Place
2. **Wave 2 (In Progress)** — Recreation centres and library branches
3. **Wave 3 (Q3 2026)** — Fire stations, transit facilities, and remote sites

### Supporting Smart City Goals

This infrastructure investment directly supports Edmonton's smart city ambitions, enabling real-time environmental monitoring, smart building management, and improved public Wi-Fi coverage.
