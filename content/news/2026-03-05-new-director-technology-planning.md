## A Warm Welcome

We are pleased to announce the appointment of our new Director of Technology Planning. This role is critical in shaping how the City of Edmonton plans, prioritizes, and invests in technology over the coming years.

### Background

Our new director brings over 15 years of experience in public sector technology leadership, with a proven track record in:

- Enterprise architecture and modernization
- Strategic IT portfolio management
- Cross-departmental digital transformation initiatives

### Vision for Technology Planning

The Technology Planning section will continue to focus on aligning technology investments with the City's strategic goals. Key priorities include:

1. **Refreshing the Technology Roadmap** — Ensuring our multi-year plan reflects current realities and emerging opportunities
2. **Strengthening Governance** — Streamlining how technology projects are evaluated, approved, and tracked
3. **Building Partnerships** — Working more closely with business units to co-create technology solutions

We look forward to the leadership and vision this appointment brings to our branch.
