## Phase 2 Complete

Project Cyber Shield, the city's flagship cybersecurity modernization initiative, has successfully completed its second phase. This milestone brings enhanced threat detection and response capabilities to all City of Edmonton departments.

### What Was Delivered

Phase 2 focused on three major deliverables:

- **Advanced Threat Detection** — Deployment of next-generation security monitoring tools across all critical infrastructure
- **Incident Response Automation** — New automated playbooks that reduce mean time to respond by 60%
- **Employee Security Training** — Completion of mandatory cybersecurity awareness training for all 13,000+ city employees

### By the Numbers

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Systems monitored | 2,400+ |
| Response time improvement | 60% faster |
| Employees trained | 13,200 |
| Phishing test pass rate | 94% |

### What's Next

Phase 3 will focus on zero-trust architecture implementation and enhanced identity management. Work is expected to begin in Q2 2026.
