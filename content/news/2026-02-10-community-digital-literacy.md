## Bridging the Digital Divide

OCT's Partner Experience section has partnered with the Edmonton Public Library to launch a city-wide digital literacy program. The initiative offers free technology workshops at library branches, targeting residents who face barriers to digital participation.

### Program Offerings

The program includes workshops on:

- **Getting Started Online** — Internet basics, email, and online safety for beginners
- **City Services Made Easy** — How to access municipal services online, from transit passes to recreation registration
- **Digital Tools for Seniors** — Tablets, video calling, and social media for staying connected
- **Job Search in the Digital Age** — Online applications, LinkedIn, and virtual interviews

### Community Impact

Early pilot sessions at the Stanley A. Milner Library showed strong demand, with all 30 seats filling within 48 hours of registration opening. Participant feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, with 96% saying they feel more confident using technology after attending.

### Get Involved

Workshops run Tuesday through Saturday at participating branches. City employees interested in volunteering as workshop facilitators can reach out to the Partner Experience team.
